This package uses Svg-cards-2.0.svg as the full-card artwork for Kindle
Aisleriot. The game overlays high-contrast corner labels for readability on
Kindle e-ink displays.

The package also includes bonded.svg from the GNOME Aisleriot data directory as
an optional "Original Bonded" card theme. GNOME/Aisleriot provenance and license
notes are documented in docs/PROVENANCE.md and NOTICE.txt.
